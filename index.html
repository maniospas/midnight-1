MIDNIGHT next morn

Midnight is the end of a civilization that fails itself.
But what comes after?


When pollution weakens temporarily, survivors emerge from bunkers. They salvage the remains of a broken world and prepare their utopia until the clock ticks again.

Access the surface only for a limited time to gather resources for your utopia. Beware of animals and the BLOO rising from blood-soaked ground.

Salvage

Civilization is rubble. Remaining resources, industry, and machines wait to be discovered. And fought over.

Command

Issue orders across the map to the living and machines. Or directly micromanage critical skirmishes. Mighty heroes will emerge too.

Research

Explore a shifting tech tree. Perhaps a little bioweapon, propaganda, or other horrors can help establish your "utopia"?
